Domestic and International Philanthropic Activities

We support Islamic Giving, Education, and other Philanthropic Activities either directly or by donation to other non-profit organizations.

A Very Lovely Welcome to our Family

Mission: Consolidate and coordinate domestic and international philanthropic activities.

Vision: Leverage individual giving by consolidating and coordinating domestic and international philanthropic activities.

We help:
  • Manage the philanthropic activities of our donors
  • Consolidate and facilitate domestic and international philanthropic activities
  • Provide opportunities for donor-participants to engage and direct domestic and international philanthropic activities
  • Identify and create new avenues for giving in the context of domestic and international philanthropic needs
  • Provide an example and legacy for the future
